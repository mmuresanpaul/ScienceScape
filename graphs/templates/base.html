Python, Sigma.js, Django, Materialize CSS, Particles.js, jQuery, FontAwesome, GitHub, Slack, Heroku, The Noun Project and Candy from Candy Mountain.

Scientometrics, reinvented

ScienceScapeS builds upon scientometrics applications such as ScienceScape, developed by M.Jacomy at the Sciences Po Médialab, Paris.

Our powerful data processing scripts are based on Sebastian Grauwin's work, Bibliotools3.0.

For detailed information about this project, visit our GitHub Repository.
